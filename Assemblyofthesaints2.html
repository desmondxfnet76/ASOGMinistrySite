About Us

The Assembly Of The Saints Of God Ministry is a five-fold, international assembly of Christ. We are a diverse group of people who come from different backgrounds and cultures, but we are united in our love for God and our desire to serve Him. We believe that the church is a place where we can grow in our faith, encourage one another, and reach out to those in need. We invite you to join us as we seek to be the hands and feet of Jesus in our community and beyond.

Pastor and First Lady

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a light in the darkness, a beacon of hope in a world that is filled with despair. We believe that the church is called to be a place of refuge and healing for those who are hurting and broken. We are committed to sharing the love of Christ with all people, regardless of their background or circumstances. We believe that the church is a place where we can find forgiveness, healing, and restoration. We invite you to join us as we seek to be the hands and feet of Jesus in our community and beyond.
